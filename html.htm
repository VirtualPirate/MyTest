Motion:-
All moving bodies that changes their position with time are in motion

Example-
  • Walking man
  • Flying Bird
  • Running Cheetah
  • All moving vehicles



Define Motion:-
A body is said to be in motion when it's position changes continuously with repect to a stationary object taken as a reference point.

For example -
When a car is moving with respext to stationary objects like house and tree, we say that the car is in motion.



Uniform Motion:-
A body has uniform motion if it travels equal distance in equal intervals of time
Examples:-
  • A car running at constatnt speed.
  • Rising Sun


Non-Uniform Motion:-
A body has non-uniform motion if it travels unqual diatances in equal intervals of time.
Example:-
If we drop a ball from the roof of a tall building, we will find that it covers unequal distances in equal intervals of time.
